About Us

About Us

Founded in 2022, Sterling Navigation Pvt.Ltd. having a team of experienced professionals with sales and technical background in Indian Surveying Industry and authorized channel partner of Unistrong Group for promoting e-Survey brand in Indian territory, delivers a comprehensive portfolio of high-performance solutions to make customers' work easier, productive, and cost-efficient.

At Sterling Navigation, we provide a complete range of products, including GNSS receivers, CORS stations, total optical stations, data collection hardware, agriculture machine control intelligent systems, rugged GIS data collectors, UAV, USV, and a wide range of software.

The e-Survey' cutting-edge hardware, software, and professional services transform multiple fields of application and industries, including building and construction, land survey, agriculture and smart farming, geospatial and more.
